Bugg

What is Bugg?

Bugg is a Swedish partner dance that is danced to music in four-four time. It is a completely improvised dance where the leader guides the follower through various spins and turns. Bugg, along with foxtrot and one-step, is one of the most common social dances in Sweden.

As a partner dance, bugg differs from many other dances because the dancers do not hold each other, as in foxtrot and waltz, for example. Instead, they hold each other with one or both hands for most of the time and frequently change grips. Bugg was traditionally danced mostly to dance band music, but nowadays it is also danced to other popular music. As long as there is a beat to follow, it is possible to dance to all kinds of music.
