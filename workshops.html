Workshops

What are our workshops?

Welcome to TLTH Dance Courses' workshops! Here you can learn various dance styles in a fun and welcoming environment. Our workshops are meant for both beginners and experienced dancers. Each week we focus on one specific dance style.

The idea of the workshops was created in 2022 when a group of us danced together in a show at the Lunda Carneval. We all had a different dance backgroud and wanted to continue dancing together while learning from one another. Wanting to give others the chance to join us in this, the workshops were created as a part of TLTH Dance Courses.

Who are we?

We are a group of students at Lund University who love to dance. While we are neither professional dancers nor professional teachers, we have all attended dance classes for the majority of our lives and want to share our knowledge and passion for dance with you.

Do you also love to dance? Join us on thursdags! If you also want to teach in our classes, please contact us on dansmastare@tlth.se.
